Welcome to Whitney Avenue

We're so thankful to God for guiding you here to consider making Whitney Avenue Church Of Christ the new 'Place Where You Belong'. We want you to feel at home here and to be part of our family. By joining Whitney Avenue Church Of Christ the doors will be opened to new relationships from a loving and caring family of belivers that strive to help you, your family as well as the surrounding community grow in the love and knowledge of the Lord Our God. Welcome! We are passionate about the Lord. Have a deep sense of love for all and our doors are open to all. Whether it be a novice to the Scripture, a seasoned saint or someone who is just beginning their spiritual journey and has doubts that Christ even existed we welcome you. Whitney Avenue Church of Christ is a safe place for the single person, couple or family to come and seek the answers you heart longs for. We serve Hamden, North Haven, and surrounding communities in the New Haven County, CT. The senior preacher of this congregation is Wilton Carraway Jr.

Introducing Pastor Carraway

Brother Wilton, also known as 'Papa Gramps' to other New England youth ministrers. Behind his large stature abides the love and gentleness of a teddy bear. After having gone astrayfrom the church for many years Brother Wiltonhas returned with a passion to spread the love of Christ which is always seen in his smile, felt in his hugs and above allheard when he speaks of Gods word.
